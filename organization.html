ORGANIZATION & ADMINISTRATION


All policies and management are determined by a body known as the Selangor Orphan Education Organization Working Party. The members of this Working Committee are appointed by individuals who are members of the Society. The members of the Workers' Committee are elected to the members by the General Assembly once every two years.
