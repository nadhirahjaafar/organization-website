WELCOME TO RUMAH BAKTI DATO'HARUN


HISTORY OF RUMAH BAKTI DATO' HARUN



In view of the responsibility and sympathy of the orphans, a group of community activists and senior officials of the Selangor State Government successfully established and registered the "Selangor Orphan Education Organization" under the Registration Number on August 16, 1966.


As With the approval of this registration, the Committee has been active in achieving its goals. On the services of Y.Bhg the late Dato 'Haji Harun Idris and the wife of Y.Bhg Datin Salmah Suleiman, the State Government of Selangor granted a 4.8 acre plot of land in Hulu Kelang (HS.231) on the approval document of PHT number.G.2 / 139 / 75. To ensure that this project can be carried out successfully Y.Bhg Dato 'Haji Harun Idris has been appointed Chairman of this Society.


On March 2, 1982 two new blocks of 2-storey dormitories were completed. The two blocks of dormitories cost RM600,000.00 and were donated by the Selangor State Government and donated by the public. A surau was also built in 1984. In 2009 a new surau was built to replace the old surau. This new prayer was contributed by Y.Bhg Datin Salmah Sulaiman, the wife of the late Dato 'Haji Harun Idris. The RBHK acquired a new library in 1989. The library became a Resource Center in 1997. Since the RBHK acquired a new surau, the old surau built in 1984 was transformed into a new Resource Center. The Resource Center is divided into two sections, namely, the library and the computer room.


In honor of the late Y.Bhg Dato 'Haji Harun Idris members of the Selangor Orphan Education Association have agreed to change the name of Hulu Kelang Hulu Klang (RBHK) to Dato' Harun (RBDH) House with the change of symbol of the Bakti House. amended and approved by the Registrar of Societies on July 29, 2011.
